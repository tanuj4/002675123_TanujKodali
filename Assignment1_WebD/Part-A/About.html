Coffee is a widely consumed beverage worldwide. This is demonstrated by the growing number of coffee franchises like Starbucks and Café Coffee Day that are located all over the world. Coffee is a beverage produced from roasted coffee beans, which are typically found in powdered form in coffee pods. Arabica, Robusta, and a combination of the two are the three varieties of coffee beans. They are grown on the Indian Peninsula, South America, and several regions of Africa.
